# Code of Conduct

This project follows the [Contributor Covenant v2.1](https://www.contributor-covenant.org/version/2/1/code_of_conduct/).

## In short

- Be respectful in issues, PRs, commits, and any other project space.
- Assume good intent; ask before judging.
- Disagree on technical decisions without making it personal.
- No harassment of any kind.

## Reporting

If you experience or witness behavior that violates this code, contact the maintainer at **lindevhard@gmail.com**. Reports are handled privately.

The full text of the covenant is linked above and governs in case of any ambiguity in the summary.
